Classic Den

A place for nerds like You!

Classic Den is a place for all kinds of nerds that are exhausted of all this pretty-looking nonsense that can be seen other than the Internet. Nowadays all websites are written to be as simple, slick, and elegant as they could be. Ugh!

Do all these people remember the Golden Age of computers?

By Golden Age, I mean of course when a Terminal was the King!

With this page, I want to bring those time back. Below you can share your thoughts, make announcements, post an ad, ask for help, and many more. Just hit the Add Entry button, type whatever you want and share with other old friends!

Each Entry will be removed after 2 days once it's published!
